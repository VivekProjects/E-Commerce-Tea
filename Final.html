About Tea



The main three teas comes from the same plant called Camellia Sinensis.

White Tea: Straight Camellia Sinensis's leaves without any processing.

Black Tea: Camellia Sinensis's leaves scraped for oxidation.

Green Tea: Camellia Sinensis's leaves steamed in water.
